Frequently Asked Questions

Our FAQ section is here to answer common questions about our services, features, and payment plans. Whether you're just getting started or considering an upgrade, you'll find helpful information to guide you in making the best choice for your business needs. If you have more questions, feel free to reach out to our support team!

1. What is included in the Basic Plan?

The Basic Plan provides limited access to core features like data analytics and single-user collaboration. It's designed for individuals or small teams looking to get started with our platform without committing to a larger plan.

2. Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time to fit your changing business needs. Simply go to your account settings to adjust your subscription, and the changes will take effect in the next billing cycle.

3. Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all new users. This allows you to explore all the features of our Standard Plan with no commitment. You can decide if our platform is the right fit before choosing a paid plan.

4. What kind of support is available with each plan?

Support options vary by plan. The Basic Plan offers email support, while the Standard and Professional Plans include 24/7 email and chat support. The Enterprise Plan provides a dedicated account manager for personalized support and assistance.

5. Is my data secure on your platform?

Absolutely. We take data security very seriously. Our platform uses advanced encryption and adheres to industry standards to ensure your data is protected at all times. Additionally, our Enterprise Plan offers enhanced security features for added peace of mind.
